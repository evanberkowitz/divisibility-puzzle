\section{Problem Statement}

We are looking for a ten digit integer $abcdefghij$ where every digit is different such that
\begin{center}
\begin{tabular}{rl}
	1	&	divides $a$,					\\
	2	&	divides $ab$,					\\
	3	&	divides $abc$,					\\
	4	&	divides $abcd$,					\\
	5	&	divides $abcde$,				\\
	6	&	divides $abcdef$,				\\
	7	&	divides $abcdefg$,				\\
	8	&	divides $abcdefgh$,				\\
	9	&	divides $abcdefghi$, and		\\
	10	&	divides $abcdefghij$.			
\end{tabular}
\end{center}
